We collect the following personal information when you create an account or book tickets:

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Customer Support: Respond to queries, resolve issues, provide assistance

3.2 We Do NOT:

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted databases with access controls
  • Payment Security: PCI DSS compliant payment processing

Note: While we take extensive measures to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and never share your account credentials.
